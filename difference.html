Our Philosophy

Less is more.

50 Words was inspired by the story behind "Green Eggs and Ham," a feat of creativity and constraint that results in simple, memorable communications and universal understanding. Dr. Seuss (Theodore Geisel) was challenged by his publisher to write an entire children's book using only 50 unique words, resulting in one of history's best-selling children's books.

Like Dr. Seuss, we believe business communication should be simple, giving you the ability to do much more—with less.

For the curious, Dr. Seuss's 50 words were: a, am, and, any, anywhere, are, be, boat, box, car, could, dark, do, eat, eggs, fox, goat, good, green, ham, here, house, I, if, in, let, like, may, me, mouse, not, on, or, rain, Sam, say, see, so, thank, that, them, there, they, train, tree, try, will, with, would, you.
