Crisis Communications

Navigate Critical Moments with Confidence

At your most critical moments, you don't have time to guess at the right strategy. Rely on our deep experience and strategy to manage transformational situations, maintain trust and maximize brand value in many different circumstances.

Representative Engagements

Strategic Communications Advisor to ONE Aviation Corporation, et al., New Mexico-based aviation entities which filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection in the United States Bankruptcy Court for the District of Delaware. Developed and executed the communications plan, created stakeholder communications, and provided ongoing communications with the media and local government officials.

Crisis Communications Advisor to New Jersey-based manufacturer in the food industry. The Company's former CEO was indicted for money laundering and defrauding the state of New Jersey. Created and implemented a crisis communications plan for all stakeholders with the Chief Restructuring Officer, Legal Counsel and other Interim Leaders, developed communication materials, provided on-site support, and provided media training.

Strategic Communications Advisor to the Court-Appointed Receiver and operator of a chain of skilled nursing facilities in the Midwestern United States. Developed media responses in coordination with the Receiver and Legal Counsel during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Strategic Communications Advisor to the Chief Executive Officer and Chief Restructuring Advisor of Center City Healthcare, LLC and certain of its subsidiaries, which filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection. Developed and executed strategic communications plans and timelines in coordination with Restructuring Counsel, the Chief Restructuring Officer and the Chief Executive Officer under a compressed timeline. Developed communications materials to 20+ stakeholder groups related to the bankruptcy and orderly wind-down of major safety-net institution Hahnemann University Hospital, a 496-bed teaching hospital in Philadelphia where more than 500 medical residents and fellows trained, and the Section 363 sale of St. Christopher's Hospital for Children, a safety-net 188-bed pediatric hospital in Philadelphia. Provided onsite support at both hospitals during the bankruptcy.

Strategic Communications Advisor to Guardian Elder Care at Johnstown, LLC d/b/a Richland Healthcare and Rehabilitation Center and certain of its affiliates, an owner and operator of skilled nursing facilities and related pharmacy and rehabilitation businesses in Pennsylvania and West Virginia, which filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection. Developed and executed strategic communications plans and timelines in coordination with the Chief Restructuring Officer, Legal Counsel, and Chief Executive Officer during the divestitures and transfers of the operations of its facilities, ensuring continued services for its residents and preserving critical healthcare jobs. Guided internal and external communications, including communications with clinical and non-clinical staff, board of directors, community partners, media, and referring institutions. Provided onsite support as needed.
