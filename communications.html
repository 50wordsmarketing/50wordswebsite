Representative Engagements

Marketing Advisor to a global industrial aerospace defense conglomerate for the launch and U.S. debut of a new tactical jet aircraft. Supported their Corporate Communications department on the jet fly-in to Washington, D.C., for a global military conference which received national television coverage. Created press and stakeholders materials and assisted with the coordination of international news outlet publication interviews with executive leaders in a compressed time frame. Coordinated on-site logistics with staff for foreign and military dignitaries.

Communications Advisor to a minority- and women-owned asphalt and aggregate manufacturing and construction firm based in Maryland. President Barack Obama spoke to a gathering of the media, employees and guests at the Company's plant with 72 hours' notice. Created media announcements, worked with the Company's principals, Company Financial Advisor and The White House communications team and managed the on-site media for the nationally televised event in an extremely compressed timeframe. Scheduled media interviews with management and updated the website and other communication platforms in real time.

Strategic Communications Advisor to the Chief Communications Officer of a major metropolitan area archdiocese related to their Independent Reconciliation and Reparations Program with a focus on transparency, clarity, and compassion. The engagement helped the diocese articulate the program's purpose and process with integrity, support informed participation, and reinforce its commitment to accountability, healing, and trust-building.

Strategic Communications Advisor to a Philadelphia-based labor relations organization of building owners and agents who own or manage over 100 million square feet of commercial properties and employ maintenance personnel who are members of the largest property services union in the United States. Created and implemented a strike communication plan and provided media training. Provided 24-hour media contact coverage during the negotiations. Engaged in successive negotiation years 2011, 2015, 2019 and 2023.
